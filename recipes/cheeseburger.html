Cheeseburger

Description

Super simple and bare bones burger.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup Veggie Vinaigrette
  • 1/4 cup plain panko bread crumbs
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon onion seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil, optional
  • 6 slices cheddar cheese
  • 6 lettuce leaves
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 6 hamburger buns
