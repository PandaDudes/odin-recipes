Dumplings

Description

Making dumplings at home is incredibly easy; the hardest part about the whole process is pleating them (which is also easy!). These dumplings have a short list of ingredients, but are nevertheless incredibly flavorful, perfect for dunking in a range of different sauces.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork shoulder or pork belly, trimmed of connective tissue, but with fat intact, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/4 pound slab bacon, cut into 1/2-inch cubes, frozen
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chicken stock, pork stock, or cold water
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 16 scallions, white and green parts, finely sliced
  • Round dumpling wrappers
